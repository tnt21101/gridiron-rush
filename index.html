07 — Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How long until my site is live? +

Starter sites go live in 7 days, Growth in 14, Scale on a custom timeline confirmed during discovery.

Do I own the website when it's done? +

Yes — fully. Domain, code, and content all transfer to you. We never lock clients in.

What if I don't have any content or photos? +

We write the copy and source professional imagery as part of the build. Custom photography is available as an add-on.

Can you redesign an existing site? +

Often. We start with an audit, preserve any SEO equity, and rebuild the rest from a clean slate.

What's in the monthly performance report? +

A single-page summary: leads, traffic sources, top pages, estimated revenue based on your close rate and customer value, and one recommended improvement for the next month.
